About Strawberry Express

We are an eco-friendly grocery delivery service connecting you directly to local farms. Our goal is simple — fresh, seasonal food delivered to your door with zero unnecessary waste.

Founded in 2025, Strawberry Express was born out of a love for nature and good food. We believe that everyone deserves access to healthy ingredients, grown responsibly and delivered sustainably.
